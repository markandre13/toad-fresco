MenuBar [index]

declared in file: /opt/Fresco/Fresco/defs/menu.idl
ancestors: Menu, Viewer

The menu bar is a viewer that can be placed in a window. It contains buttons to access pulldown menus. . When a menu bar needs to update its appearance because of a change in the menuitem hierarchy, its need_resize function should be called.
