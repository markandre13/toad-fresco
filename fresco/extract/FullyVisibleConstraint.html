FullyVisibleConstraint [index]

declared in file: /opt/Fresco/Fresco/defs/layouts.idl
ancestors: AdjustmentConstraint

This constraint can be configured to control the scrolling region when the view is larger than the data. When the view is smaller than the data the scrolling region is always constrained to show as much of the data as possible.
float usable;
The usable attribute controls how much of the white space can be used for scrolling when the view is larger than the data.
float align;
The align attribute controls the alignment of the data in the view when the view is larger than the data.
