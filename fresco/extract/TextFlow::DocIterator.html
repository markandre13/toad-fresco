Navigation navigation interface for some portion of a document. some kind of way.

next and prev move the iterator forward and backward in some way that is determined by the implementation. A document might hand out a number of different kinds of iterators that allow the iterator to be positioned in certain places while ignoring others. valid() determines if the iterator is still positioned within its range of responsibility. is_first() and is_last() determine if the iterator is positioned over the begin/end of the iterators range of responsibility. reset_to_first() and reset_to_last() will position the iterator at the begin/end of the range of responsibility.
Subject get_subject();
get_subject returns the document associated with the iterator. get_element returns the document element that the iterator is currently positioned to.
Range span;
portion of the document that the iterator is responsible for.
